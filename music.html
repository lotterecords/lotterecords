Monsieur le Duc

Slapjack

Monsieur le Duc is back with a new album, "an album that pukes on you", an album called Slapjack. The Duke is ever-obsessed with not lifting the veil, so his new work is a sort of departure for him, as he attempts to show a more tender and personal side "found at the bottom of a maze of foxholes", a softer side, "a sort of soul, caught in rubber cement."

European Vacation

Join le Duc for a few brief rounds of double dutch around the Old World. Together, learn French, German, Spanish, Italian and even a little compassion with the reclusive copycat! Inspired by the fragrant droppings of Brezel Göring, the explosions of Richard Mortensen, and the execution of Chevy Chase, European Vacation leaves one absolutely exhausted, perfectly simulating the malaise which follows one's travels abroad!
